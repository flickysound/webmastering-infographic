Career Paths:

Webmaster

Definition

A webmaster is someone who creates and manages the content on a website, or manages the computer server and technical programming aspects of a website, or does both.

Requirements

  • Proven experience as a Webmaster or Web Developer
  • Knowledge of web analytics and SEO
  • Familiarity with web standards
  • Working knowledge of website management tools
  • Proficient in HTML/CSS, XML, SQL, and Javascript
  • Strong troubleshooting and analytical abilities
  • Excellent communication and teamwork skills
  • Ability to generate creative ideas
  • Attention to detail
  • Organizational skills
  • Bachelor of Science or Bachelor of Arts in Computer Science, Design or a related field

Salary

The median annual salary is $87,800. Salary generally increases annually. The lowest annual salary is $67,740 and the highest is $110,916. Education makes a slight difference in salary.
